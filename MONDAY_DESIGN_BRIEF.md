## 1.2 Typography

### Display / Hero Font
**Font:** `Space Grotesk` (Google Fonts)
- Weights used: 700 (Bold), 800 (ExtraBold)
- Used for: Headlines, token name, hero copy
- Personality: Techy, modern, slightly quirky — feels like a terminal crossed with a billboard
- Letter-spacing: -0.02em on large sizes for tighter, commanding feel
- Google Fonts URL: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Space+Grotesk

### Body Font
**Font:** `Inter` (Google Fonts)
- Weights used: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (SemiBold)
- Used for: Body copy, descriptions, captions, UI text
- Personality: Neutral, readable, universally trusted
- Line-height: 1.6 for body, 1.3 for UI elements
- Google Fonts URL: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter

### Accent Font
**Font:** `Bebas Neue` (Google Fonts)
- Weights used: 400 (Regular — it's all-caps by design)
- Used for: Meme text overlays, big stat numbers, rally cries
- Personality: Loud, urgent, poster-like — perfect for meme formatting
- Letter-spacing: 0.05em
- Google Fonts URL: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Bebas+Neue

## 1.3 Logo Design

### Concept
The $MONDAY logo combines a **crescent moon drooping like a tired eye** with the letter **M** — the M is formed by two peaks that read as tired eyelids or a jagged heartbeat monitor flatline with a bump. The icon is bold, simple, and instantly readable at 16px.

### Icon/Symbol: "The Drooping M"
**Primary mark:** A stylized capital **M** where:
- The two inner diagonals droop downward like heavy eyelids (not sharp peaks, slightly concave)
- A small crescent moon hangs below the right leg of the M, like a sleepy moon badge
- The entire icon sits inside a **rounded square** (superellipse, border-radius ≈ 22% of width)
- Background fill: **Monday Blue** `#1A1F3C`
- M letterform: **Alarm Yellow** `#FFD60A`
- Crescent: **Cold Fog** `#8892A4` (muted, like a tired moon)
- A single dark circle (pupil-like dot) sits in the center belly of the M to imply a sleepy face

**Style:** Flat design with minimal shadow. Bold outlines. Cartoon-adjacent but geometric — think between Doge's simplicity and Solana's polish.

**Stroke:** M has a 3–4px white inner glow to separate from the dark background at small sizes.

### Logo Variations
| Variant | Description | Use Case |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Icon Mark** | Rounded square, M + crescent | Favicon, Twitter pfp, app icon |
| **Horizontal Wordmark** | Icon left + "$MONDAY" right in Space Grotesk 800 | Website header, banners |
| **Stacked Wordmark** | Icon above "$MONDAY" | Profile bios, square formats |
| **Dark Background** | Yellow M on `#1A1F3C` square | Default — all dark contexts |
| **Light Background** | Dark M (`#1A1F3C`) on `#F0EEF8` square | Press kits, light context only |
| **Monochrome** | All white | Embossed merch, watermarks |
| **Glitch Edition** | Icon with RGB chromatic aberration offset | Special announcements, big news |

### Small Size Behavior
- At 32×32px: Only the M silhouette and crescent are visible — no text
- At 64×64px: M + crescent + subtle glow
- At 128×128px+: Full detail including pupil dot
- The rounded square container ensures recognizability in circular crop (Twitter, Discord)
- Test at 16px favicon: the M peaks must remain distinct — bump up the M line weight if needed

# DELIVERABLE 2: MASCOT DESIGN BRIEF

## 2.1 Character Overview: **MON**

**Name:** MON (pronounced "Mohn" — like Monday, like a moan)
**Nickname options:** "The Mon", "Little Mon", "Monday Mon"
**Species:** An indeterminate small creature — visually closest to a **raccoon/tanuki hybrid**, but simplified to near-abstraction. NOT a cat (Garfield), NOT a frog (Pepe), NOT a bear (Pedobear), NOT a dog (Doge).

### Why Raccoon/Tanuki?
- Raccoons have universally "tired" eyes (natural dark circles)
- Associated with scavenging, survival, night-dwelling — Monday energy personified
- Tanuki (Japanese raccoon dog) has crypto cultural resonance and is less Western-IP-loaded
- The round, fluffy silhouette reads perfectly at 32×32px
- Has "bandit mask" natural markings = the dark circles of someone who didn't sleep

---

## 2.2 Character Design Specifications

### Body
- **Shape:** Chubby, round body. Wide and low. Like a sandbag that gave up.
- **Height ratio:** Head = 60% of total height. Classic cartoon proportions for memeability.
- **Limbs:** Short, stubby arms and legs. Can barely reach a coffee mug. That's fine. He's tired.
- **Tail:** Thick raccoon-style ringed tail, always dragging on the floor (never perky)
- **Fur colors:**
  - Main body: `#4A3728` (dark warm brown)
  - Belly/chest: `#C8A882` (lighter tan)
  - Face mask (raccoon circles): `#1A1F3C` (Monday Blue — the dark circles are literally brand color)
  - Inner ear: `#FF8FAB` (soft pink — adds warmth so he's not too dark/scary)
  - Tail rings: alternating `#4A3728` and `#8B6914` (muted gold)

### Face
- **Eyes:** Large, heavy-lidded, half-closed. The top eyelid hangs at the 60% line. Whites are slightly bloodshot (tiny red lines radiating from iris). Pupils: small, tired, slightly unfocused. Color: deep amber `#B8860B`.
- **Eyebrows:** Thick. Always furrowed in the center, slightly raised on outer edges — the universal "I can't believe it's Monday" expression. Eyebrow color: `#1A1F3C` (dark, prominent)
- **Nose:** Small black oval, slightly shiny highlight dot
- **Mouth:** Default expression — a flat, downward-curved line. Not a frown, just... nothing. The expression of someone who has accepted their fate.
- **Ears:** Rounded raccoon ears, slightly drooping outward (not alert/upright)
- **Cheeks:** Subtle blush circles in `#FF8FAB` — soft, even when angry. This is what gives him warmth.

### Signature Physical Details (what makes MON ownable)
1. **The Eternal Bedhead:** Two tufts of fur always stick up from the top of his head, no matter what. Even when celebrating. Even when sleeping. They are always there.
2. **The Monday Mark:** A small stylized crescent moon birthmark on his left cheek (ties to logo)
3. **The Forever Frown Wrinkle:** A single worry line between his eyebrows. Always visible.
4. **The Raccoon Dark Circles:** His natural eye mask is the brand's Monday Blue — intentional.
5. **The Dragging Tail:** Tail always appears heavy, ringed, trailing behind him limply.

## 2.3 Signature Poses

### Pose 1: DEFAULT
- Standing upright (barely)
- Arms at sides, slightly hanging forward like a zombie
- Eyes half-closed, expressionless flat mouth
- One ear slightly drooped more than the other
- Tail dragging behind
- Vibe: "I exist. I regret it."

### Pose 2: COFFEE
- Holding an oversized mug (mug is nearly as big as his body)
- Mug reads: "$MON" in Bebas Neue on the side
- Both hands wrapped around mug, face buried in steam
- Eyes closed (first moment of peace he's had)
- Steam swirls form tiny dollar signs and crescent moons
- Tail curled around his feet for warmth
- Vibe: "This is the only reason I am alive."

### Pose 3: CRYING
- Face crumpled, mouth open in a wide, ugly cry (the "not cute crying" cry)
- Tears not single elegant drops — full rivers streaming down face, pooling at feet
- Arms raised slightly, hands open — helpless gesture
- Eyes squeezed shut, eyebrows arched up dramatically
- Bedhead tufts wilting slightly
- Vibe: "Portfolio. Alarm. Monday. All at once."

### Pose 4: RAGE
- Full body lean forward, fists clenched at sides
- Eyes WIDE OPEN for the first time — bloodshot, intense
- Mouth open showing teeth — not scary, still cute, but ANGRY
- Forehead vein visible (small zigzag line)
- Steam rising from top of head (not from coffee this time)
- Tail puffed up and bristling
- Small fires or lightning bolts floating around him
- Vibe: "It's Monday AND the gas fees are high."

### Pose 5: SLEEPING
- Fully horizontal, face-down, arms stretched forward like fell asleep mid-walk
- One eye cracked open (in case of opportunity)
- "Zzz" bubbles floating up in crescent moon shapes
- Small puddle of drool
- Phone visible near his hand — screen shows "MONDAY 07:00 — ALARM" notification
- Vibe: "Five more minutes. Five more minutes was 3 hours ago."

### Pose 6: MOON
- Standing upright but leaning back in amazement/celebration
- One arm raised pointing at a large crescent moon above
- Mouth open in a huge grin — rare! This is the only pose where he's happy
- Eyes WIDE with wonder (the whites show above the iris for once)
- His dark circles glow slightly (bioluminescent with gains)
- Bags of coins floating around him, tiny Solana logo visible on coins
- Stars and moon in background
- Tail held up high — the ONE time it's not dragging
- Vibe: "I've been waiting my whole miserable life for this."

### Pose 7: BONUS — GUN (POINTING)
- Side profile, pointing finger-gun
- One eye squinted (aiming)
- Used for "this project" meme format
- Slight smirk — rare expression, slightly unhinged

### Pose 8: BONUS — PRESENTING
- Standing beside a whiteboard or chart
- Holding a pointer stick
- Chart shows only one direction: up
- Classic "well, actually" pose
- Used for announcements, tokenomics reveals

## 2.4 Style Reference
- **Simplicity level:** Pepe the Frog (3–4 colors, flat shapes, immediately recognizable at thumbnail)
- **Warmth level:** Doge meme (the blush circles and soft browns make him lovable despite being grumpy)
- **Energy level:** Pudgy Penguins (round, chubby, marketable, merch-ready silhouette)
- **Cultural vibe:** Wojak (relatable despair) but with a mascot body instead of a face
- **NOT:** Anime (too niche), realistic (too detailed), horror (too alienating)

### What Makes MON Original & Ownable
1. The raccoon/tanuki species is underused in crypto mascots
2. The natural dark circles = brand colors integration is unique and smart
3. The crescent moon birthmark ties mascot to logo
4. The "bedhead tufts" are a signature silhouette element — recognizable in black silhouette alone
5. The consistently dragging tail creates a distinctive ground-level silhouette
6. The "one good thing = coffee" personality is universally relatable
7. He's grumpy but never mean — angry at Mondays, not at you. This is important for mass appeal.
